Features

Built for real families.

No surveillance. No complexity. Just a system that works — and kids who feel proud when they unlock it themselves.

🔒

Privacy first

TutorUnlock collects zero personal data. No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking. Everything stays on your device.

⚙️

Fully configurable

Set daily learning goals, fun time rewards, and an optional daily cap. 1.5 minutes of fun earned per minute of learning — or set your own ratio.

📊

Parent dashboard

See learning progress at a glance. Grant bonus fun time, reset today's progress, or review time-spent reports — all in one place.

🔄

Resets daily at 4 AM

Goals reset every morning. Yesterday's session doesn't carry over — a clean slate, every day. Change the reset hour in settings.
